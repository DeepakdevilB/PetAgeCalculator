Dental health is one of the most overlooked aspects of pet care. By age 3, the vast majority of dogs and cats have some evidence of periodontal disease.

Bacteria from inflamed gums can enter your pet's bloodstream and cause permanent damage to their heart, liver, and kidneys. Daily brushing and regular veterinary cleanings can literally add years to their life.
