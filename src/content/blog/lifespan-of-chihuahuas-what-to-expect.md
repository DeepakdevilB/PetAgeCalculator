Chihuahuas are known for their tiny size and massive personalities. They are also famous for being one of the longest-living dog breeds in the world. 

It's not uncommon for a healthy Chihuahua to live well past 15 years, and some even reach 20! However, they are prone to dental issues and heart conditions, so preventative vet care is essential.
