Golden Retrievers are beloved family pets, but their large size makes them susceptible to joint issues as they age. Once a Golden Retriever reaches age 7 or 8, they are officially considered a senior.

Investing in orthopedic beds, adjusting their exercise routine, and consulting your vet about omega-3 and glucosamine supplements are crucial steps to keeping your Golden comfortable.
