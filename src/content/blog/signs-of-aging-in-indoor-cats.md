As cats transition into their senior years, their behavior and physical needs change. Because cats are naturally stoic, it can be difficult to notice when they start experiencing age-related discomfort. 

Look out for changes in their jumping ability, increased vocalization, or changes in their grooming habits. Early detection of age-related issues can significantly improve your cat's quality of life in their golden years.
