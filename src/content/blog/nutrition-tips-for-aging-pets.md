One of the biggest mistakes pet owners make is feeding their senior pets the same high-calorie kibble they ate as adults. As pets age, their energy requirements decrease significantly.

Continuing to feed them the same portion sizes will almost certainly lead to obesity, which exacerbates joint pain and heart issues. Switch to a senior-specific diet that is lower in calories but highly digestible, with added supplements for joint and brain health.
